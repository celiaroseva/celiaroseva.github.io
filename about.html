Hi, I'm Celia Rose

I am a recent college graduate living in Raleigh, NC. When I’m not assisting small businesses and entrepreneurs, I love to spend my time baking sweet treats!

I received my degree in Communications Studies with a concentration in Media and Technology Studies and Production from The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. After college, I decided that I wanted to put my studies to good use and start my own business. I was lucky to have heard about the virtual assistant field from Instagram, so I thought I’d look more into it. What I found was that it sounded right up my alley! I love that it allows me to work from home, be creative, and work with inspiring people.

Time-freedom is one of the most important qualities I was looking for in a career. What I love about being a VA is that I not only have the ability to control my own time, but I am also helping others free up their busy schedules. I want to help others focus on their businesses and be able to devote their time to the things that they love. Delegation allows you to get your work-life balance back. I am a big fan of the “work smarter not harder” saying. It’s a cliché - but for good reason!

If you are interested in working with me, feel free to fill out my inquiry form! I cannot wait to work with you, and I know that we can add a little more peace, joy, and clarity into your endeavors!
